Intro & Brief

Welcome to my footwear design portfolio. This collection represents over 25 years of experience in footwear design, pattern making, and product development. Throughout my career, I have been committed to creating footwear that combines innovation, functionality, and craftsmanship.

From the initial concept to final production, each project reflects my passion for solving design challenges while balancing performance, comfort, and aesthetics. This portfolio showcases a selection of my work across athletic footwear, military boots, and lifestyle shoes developed for both local and international manufacturers.

I believe every shoe tells a story, and every design begins with a purpose.

Thank you for taking the time to explore my work. Every project in this portfolio represents my dedication to quality, innovation, and continuous improvement in footwear design. I look forward to creating products that inspire, perform, and make a lasting impact in the footwear industry.

Reynaldo Perez

Footwear Designer | Pattern Maker | Product Developer
