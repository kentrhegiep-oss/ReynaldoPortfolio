Bio

Reynaldo Perez is an experienced Footwear Designer with over 15 years of expertise in footwear design, pattern making, and product development. Throughout his career, he has successfully transformed concepts into commercially produced footwear by combining creative design with technical precision.

Reynaldo has worked with leading footwear companies and manufacturers in the Philippines and China, specializing in athletic footwear, running shoes, training footwear, and military boots. His expertise spans the entire product development process—from concept sketching and pattern making to sample development, material selection, manufacturing, and commercialization.

Passionate about innovation and craftsmanship, Reynaldo continues to create functional, performance-driven footwear that meets both market trends and manufacturing standards while maintaining exceptional quality.
